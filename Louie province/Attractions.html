Mountain Province in the Philippines is known for its stunning natural landscapes, rich culture, and unique attractions. Some of the top attractions in Mountain Province include:


  • Banaue Rice Terraces: Often called the "Eighth Wonder of the World," these ancient terraces were carved into the mountains over 2,000 years ago by the Ifugao people.


  • Sagada: Known for its hanging coffins, caves, and beautiful hiking trails, Sagada offers a unique cultural and outdoor experience.


  • Mt. Province Museum: Located in Bontoc, this museum showcases the culture, history, and artifacts of the various tribes in the region.

  • Sumaguing Cave: One of the most popular caves in Sagada, known for its impressive limestone formations and underground rivers.


  • Maligcong Rice Terraces: A lesser-known but equally stunning set of rice terraces in Bontoc, known for their beauty and intricate irrigation system.


  • Mount Amuyao: Offers a challenging trek with stunning views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.


  • Lumiang Burial Cave: Known for its collection of ancient coffins stacked inside the cave, offering a glimpse into the burial traditions of the local tribes.

    • These are just a few of the many attractions that make Mountain Province a unique and memorable destination.
